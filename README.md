# CodeAlpha Biotechnology Internship — Task 2: Case Study Analysis

## 💉 Moderna: Pioneering the mRNA Platform

A case study presentation prepared for **Task 2** of the CodeAlpha Biotechnology Virtual Internship.

## 📊 About

A 12-slide presentation analyzing Moderna as a real-world biotechnology company, covering:

- **Innovation** — the mRNA platform technology and how it works (design, delivery, instruction, response)
- **Flagship product** — Spikevax, its development timeline, efficacy, and sales trajectory
- **Applications** — the expanding pipeline into respiratory vaccines, oncology, rare disease, and autoimmune conditions
- **Market impact** — revenue growth from $803M (2020) to an $18.5B pandemic peak (2021), global dose delivery, and market positioning
- **Challenges** — the post-pandemic revenue decline, competitive pressure, regulatory risk, and path to profitability
- **Outlook** — company targets through 2026–2028, including oncology pipeline readouts and cash-breakeven goals
